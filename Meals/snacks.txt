SNACK MEALS

[Normal]

1. Apple slices with peanut butter
   Calories: 180
   Macros: 4g protein, 6g fat, 28g carbs, 3g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 1 medium apple, sliced
   - 2 tablespoons peanut butter
   - Optional: cinnamon for sprinkling
   Preparation:
   1. Wash and slice the apple into wedges
   2. Spread peanut butter on each slice
   3. Sprinkle with cinnamon if desired
   Difficulty: Very Easy
   Prep time: 5 minutes
   Dietary notes: Suitable for normal snack plans

2. Greek yogurt with honey and almonds
   Calories: 250
   Macros: 15g protein, 10g fat, 20g carbs, 2g fiber

   Preparation:

   2. Drizzle with honey
   3. Top with sliced almonds
   Difficulty: Very Easy
   Prep time: 3 minutes
   Dietary notes: Suitable for normal snack plans
