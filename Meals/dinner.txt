1. Grilled salmon with quinoa and steamed vegetables
   Calories: 500
   Macros: 35g protein, 20g fat, 25g carbs, 4g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 6 oz salmon fillet
   - 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
   - 1 cup mixed steamed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, zucchini)
   - 1 tbsp olive oil
   - 2 cloves garlic, minced
   - Fresh herbs (dill, parsley)
   - Lemon wedges
   Preparation:
   1. Season salmon with salt, pepper, and herbs.
   2. Grill salmon for 4-5 minutes per side until cooked through.
   3. Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
   4. Steam vegetables until tender-crisp.
   5. Serve salmon over quinoa with vegetables and lemon wedges.
   Difficulty: Medium
   Prep time: 25–30 minutes
   Dietary notes: Balanced dinner for normal preferences

2. Chicken stir-fry with brown rice
   Calories: 500
   Macros: 35g protein, 20g fat, 25g carbs, 4g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 6 oz chicken breast, sliced
   - 1/2 cup cooked brown rice
   - 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, snap peas, carrots, onions)
   - 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
   - 1 tbsp sesame oil
   - 2 cloves garlic, minced
   - 1 tsp ginger, grated
   Preparation:
   1. Cook brown rice according to package instructions.
   2. Heat oil in a wok or large pan over medium-high heat.
   3. Stir-fry chicken until no longer pink, about 5-6 minutes.
   4. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until crisp-tender.
   5. Add garlic, ginger, and soy sauce, stir to combine.
   6. Serve over brown rice.
   Difficulty: Medium
   Prep time: 25–30 minutes
   Dietary notes: Balanced dinner for normal preferences

11. Paneer tikka with sautéed spinach
   Calories: 480
   Macros: 25g protein, 18g fat, 30g carbs, 4g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 8 oz paneer, cubed
   - 2 cups fresh spinach
   - 1 tbsp olive oil
   - 2 cloves garlic, minced
   - 1 small onion, diced
   - 2 tbsp tikka masala paste
   - 1/4 cup plain yogurt
   - 1/2 lemon, juiced
   - Fresh cilantro for garnish
   Preparation:
   1. Marinate paneer in yogurt and tikka masala paste for 15 minutes.
   2. Thread paneer onto skewers and grill for 2-3 minutes per side.
   3. In a separate pan, heat oil and sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
   4. Add spinach and cook until wilted, about 2 minutes.
   5. Season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
   6. Serve paneer tikka over sautéed spinach, garnished with cilantro.
   Difficulty: Medium
   Prep time: 25–30 minutes
   Dietary notes: Balanced dinner for vegetarian preferences

21. Quinoa and black bean chili
   Calories: 450
   Macros: 18g protein, 12g fat, 65g carbs, 15g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 1 cup cooked quinoa
   - 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
   - 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
   - 1 bell pepper, diced
   - 1 onion, diced
   - 2 cloves garlic, minced
   - 1 tbsp olive oil
   - 2 tbsp chili powder
   - 1 tsp cumin
   - 1/2 tsp paprika
   - Fresh cilantro and avocado for garnish
   Preparation:
   1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
   2. Sauté onion, bell pepper, and garlic until softened, about 5 minutes.
   3. Add spices and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
   4. Add tomatoes, black beans, and 1 cup water. Bring to a simmer.
   5. Cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
   6. Stir in cooked quinoa and heat through.
   7. Serve topped with cilantro and avocado slices.
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 30–35 minutes
   Dietary notes: High-fiber, plant-based dinner for vegan preferences

31. Grilled salmon with roasted asparagus
   Calories: 450
   Macros: 35g protein, 25g fat, 15g carbs, 5g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 6 oz salmon fillet
   - 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
   - 2 tbsp olive oil
   - 2 cloves garlic, minced
   - 1 lemon, half sliced and half juiced
   - 1 tsp dried herbs (dill, thyme)
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   Preparation:
   1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
   2. Toss asparagus with 1 tbsp olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
   3. Arrange on a baking sheet and roast for 12-15 minutes until tender.
   4. Meanwhile, season salmon with herbs, salt, and pepper.
   5. Heat remaining oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
   6. Cook salmon skin-side down for 4 minutes, then flip and cook for 2-3 minutes more.
   7. Drizzle with lemon juice and serve with roasted asparagus and lemon slices.
   Difficulty: Medium
   Prep time: 25–30 minutes
   Dietary notes: Naturally gluten-free dinner rich in omega-3 fatty acids

41. Grilled chicken with roasted Brussels sprouts
   Calories: 400
   Macros: 35g protein, 25g fat, 10g carbs, 4g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 6 oz chicken breast
   - 2 cups Brussels sprouts, halved
   - 2 tbsp olive oil
   - 2 cloves garlic, minced
   - 1 tsp dried herbs (thyme, rosemary)
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   Preparation:

60. Keto chili with ground beef and cheddar cheese
   Calories: 520
   Macros: 35g protein, 40g fat, 10g carbs, 4g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 1 1/2 lbs ground beef (80/20)
   - 1 medium onion, diced
   - 2 bell peppers, diced
   - 3 cloves garlic, minced
   - 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
   - 2 tbsp tomato paste
   - 2 tbsp olive oil
   - 2 tbsp chili powder
   - 1 tbsp cumin
   - 1 tsp paprika
   - 1/2 tsp oregano
   - 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
   - 1/4 cup sour cream
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   Preparation:
   1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.

   10. Serve topped with shredded cheddar cheese and a dollop of sour cream.

   Prep time: 35–40 minutes
   Dietary notes: Hearty, high-fat keto chili without beans
