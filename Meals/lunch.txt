LUNCH MEALS

[Normal]

1. Grilled chicken with quinoa and roasted vegetables
   Calories: 475
   Macros: 30g protein, 12g fat, 45g carbs, 6g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 6 oz chicken breast
   - 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, zucchini, carrots)
   - 1/2 cup quinoa, uncooked
   - 1 tbsp olive oil
   - Salt, pepper, and herbs to taste
   Preparation:
   1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
   2. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
   3. Grill chicken until internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
   4. Toss vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then roast for 20 minutes.
   5. Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
   6. Combine and serve warm.
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 20 minutes
   Dietary notes: Balanced meal option based on normal diet

2. Brown rice with stir-fried tofu and mixed veggies
   Calories: 437
   Macros: 18g protein, 10g fat, 60g carbs, 8g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 6 oz firm tofu, cubed
   - 1 cup mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, snap peas)
   - 1/2 cup brown rice, uncooked
   - 1 tbsp sesame oil
   - 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
   - 1 tsp ginger, minced
   - 1 clove garlic, minced
   Preparation:
   1. Cook brown rice according to package instructions.
   2. Heat sesame oil in a wok over medium-high heat.
   3. Add tofu and cook until golden, about 5 minutes.
   4. Add vegetables, garlic, and ginger, stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
   5. Add soy sauce and combine with rice.
   6. Serve warm.
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 20 minutes
   Dietary notes: Balanced meal option based on normal diet, quick

3. Tuna salad sandwich on whole grain bread
   Calories: 415
   Macros: 25g protein, 14g fat, 35g carbs, 5g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 1 can (5 oz) tuna in water, drained
   - 2 tbsp light mayonnaise
   - 1/4 cup celery, diced
   - 1/4 cup red onion, diced
   - 2 slices whole grain bread
   - 1 leaf lettuce
   - 1 slice tomato
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   Preparation:
   1. Mix tuna, mayonnaise, celery, and onion in a bowl.
   2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
   3. Toast bread if desired.
   4. Layer lettuce and tomato on bread.
   5. Add tuna mixture and top with second slice of bread.
   6. Serve cold.
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 20 minutes
   Dietary notes: Balanced meal option based on normal diet, quick

4. Lentil soup with whole wheat bread
   Calories: 356
   Macros: 18g protein, 8g fat, 50g carbs, 12g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 1/2 cup dry lentils
   - 1/2 cup carrots, diced
   - 1/2 cup celery, diced
   - 1/4 cup onion, diced
   - 2 cups vegetable broth
   - 1 tbsp olive oil
   - 1 clove garlic, minced
   - 1 slice whole wheat bread
   - 1 tsp cumin
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   Preparation:
   1. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat.
   2. Sauté onions, carrots, celery, and garlic until soft.
   3. Add lentils, broth, cumin, salt, and pepper.
   4. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 25-30 minutes until lentils are tender.
   5. Serve hot with whole wheat bread on the side.
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 20 minutes
   Dietary notes: Balanced meal option based on normal diet

5. Baked salmon with mashed potatoes and steamed broccoli
   Calories: 382
   Macros: 28g protein, 15g fat, 25g carbs, 3g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 5 oz salmon fillet
   - 1/2 cup potatoes, peeled and cubed
   - 1 cup broccoli florets
   - 1 tbsp olive oil
   - 1 tbsp milk or plant-based alternative
   - 1 tsp butter or plant-based alternative
   - 1 lemon wedge
   - Salt, pepper, and dill to taste
   Preparation:
   1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
   2. Season salmon with salt, pepper, and dill.
   3. Bake salmon for 15-18 minutes until it flakes easily.
   4. Boil potatoes until tender, then mash with milk and butter.
   5. Steam broccoli until bright green and tender.
   6. Serve with lemon wedge.
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 20 minutes
   Dietary notes: Balanced meal option based on normal diet

6. Chicken and vegetable stir-fry with brown rice
   Calories: 489
   Macros: 30g protein, 12g fat, 45g carbs, 5g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 6 oz chicken breast, sliced
   - 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snow peas)
   - 1/2 cup brown rice, uncooked
   - 1 tbsp olive oil
   - 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
   - 1 tsp ginger, minced
   - 1 clove garlic, minced
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   Preparation:
   1. Cook brown rice according to package instructions.
   2. Heat olive oil in a wok or large pan over medium-high heat.
   3. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink, about 5-6 minutes.
   4. Add vegetables, garlic, and ginger, stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until crisp-tender.
   5. Add soy sauce and combine with rice.
   6. Serve warm.
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 20 minutes
   Dietary notes: Balanced meal option based on normal diet, quick

7. Pasta with lean beef and marinara sauce
   Calories: 453
   Macros: 25g protein, 10g fat, 50g carbs, 6g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 4 oz lean ground beef (90% lean)
   - 1/2 cup whole wheat pasta, uncooked
   - 1/2 cup marinara sauce
   - 1/4 cup onion, diced
   - 1/4 cup bell pepper, diced
   - 1 clove garlic, minced
   - 1 tsp olive oil
   - 1 tsp Italian seasoning
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   - Optional: 1 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
   Preparation:
   1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
   2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
   3. Add onion, bell pepper, and garlic, sauté for 2-3 minutes.
   4. Add ground beef and cook until browned, about 5 minutes.
   5. Drain excess fat if necessary.
   6. Add marinara sauce and Italian seasoning, simmer for 5 minutes.
   7. Combine with pasta and serve warm.
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 20 minutes
   Dietary notes: Balanced meal option based on normal diet

8. Chickpea and spinach curry with whole grain roti
   Calories: 499
   Macros: 18g protein, 12g fat, 70g carbs, 14g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 1/2 cup chickpeas, cooked
   - 2 cups fresh spinach
   - 1 small onion, diced
   - 1 tomato, diced
   - 1 clove garlic, minced
   - 1/2 tsp ginger, grated
   - 1 tbsp olive oil
   - 1 tsp curry powder
   - 1/2 tsp cumin
   - 1/4 tsp turmeric
   - 2 whole grain roti
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   Preparation:
   1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
   2. Add onion, garlic, and ginger, sauté until soft.
   3. Add spices and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
   4. Add tomatoes and cook for 2-3 minutes until softened.
   5. Add chickpeas and 1/4 cup water, simmer for 5 minutes.
   6. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
   7. Serve with whole grain roti.
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 20 minutes
   Dietary notes: Balanced meal option based on normal diet

9. Turkey and avocado wrap with a side salad
   Calories: 444
   Macros: 25g protein, 18g fat, 35g carbs, 8g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 4 oz sliced turkey breast
   - 1/2 avocado, sliced
   - 1 whole wheat tortilla (8-inch)
   - 1 leaf lettuce
   - 2 slices tomato
   - 1 tbsp hummus
   - 1 cup mixed greens
   - 1/2 cup cucumber, sliced
   - 1/2 tbsp olive oil
   - 1/2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   Preparation:
   1. Spread hummus on tortilla.
   2. Layer turkey, avocado, lettuce, and tomato on tortilla.
   3. Roll up tightly and cut in half.
   4. Toss mixed greens and cucumber with olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
   5. Serve wrap with side salad.
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 20 minutes
   Dietary notes: Balanced meal option based on normal diet, quick

10. Stuffed bell peppers with ground turkey and rice
   Calories: 475
   Macros: 25g protein, 12g fat, 40g carbs, 6g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 4 oz lean ground turkey
   - 2 bell peppers, halved and seeded
   - 1/4 cup brown rice, uncooked
   - 1/4 cup onion, diced
   - 1/4 cup tomato sauce
   - 1 clove garlic, minced
   - 1 tsp olive oil
   - 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
   - 1/4 cup low-fat cheese, shredded (optional)
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   Preparation:
   1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
   2. Cook rice according to package instructions.
   3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
   4. Add onion and garlic, sauté until soft.
   5. Add ground turkey and cook until browned.
   6. Mix in cooked rice, tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
   7. Fill bell pepper halves with mixture.
   8. Top with cheese if desired.
   9. Bake for 25-30 minutes until peppers are tender.
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 20 minutes
   Dietary notes: Balanced meal option based on normal diet

31. Grilled chicken with roasted vegetables
   Calories: 377
   Macros: 30g protein, 12g fat, 20g carbs, 5g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 6 oz chicken breast
   - 1 cup mixed vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, carrots)
   - 1 tbsp olive oil
   - 1 tsp dried herbs (rosemary, thyme)
   - 1 clove garlic, minced
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   Preparation:
   1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
   2. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and half the herbs.
   3. Grill chicken until internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
   4. Toss vegetables with olive oil, garlic, remaining herbs, salt, and pepper.
   5. Roast vegetables for 20-25 minutes until tender.
   6. Serve chicken with roasted vegetables.
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 30 minutes
   Dietary notes: Balanced meal option based on gluten-free diet

51. Grilled steak with buttered spinach
   Calories: 475
   Macros: 35g protein, 30g fat, 5g carbs, 2g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 6 oz ribeye or sirloin steak
   - 2 cups fresh spinach
   - 1 tbsp butter
   - 1 clove garlic, minced
   - 1/2 tbsp olive oil
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   Preparation:
   1. Season steak with salt and pepper.
   2. Heat olive oil in a pan over high heat.
   3. Cook steak to desired doneness (3-4 minutes per side for medium-rare).
   4. In another pan, melt butter over medium heat.
   5. Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds, then add spinach.
   6. Cook spinach until just wilted, about 2 minutes.
   7. Serve steak with buttered spinach.
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 15 minutes
   Dietary notes: High-fat, low-carb meal option based on keto diet
