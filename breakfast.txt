BREAKFAST MEALS

[Normal]

1. Scrambled Eggs with Whole Wheat Toast
   Calories: 320
   Macros: 18g protein, 15g fat, 25g carbs, 4g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 2 large eggs
   - 1 slice whole wheat bread
   - 1 tsp butter
   - 1 tbsp milk
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   Preparation:
   1. Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper
   2. Melt butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat
   3. Pour in egg mixture and stir gently until cooked but still moist
   4. Toast bread until golden brown
   5. Serve eggs on or alongside toast
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 10 minutes
   Dietary notes: High protein, can be made dairy-free by omitting milk and using oil instead of butter

2. Oatmeal with Banana and Honey
   Calories: 350
   Macros: 8g protein, 5g fat, 70g carbs, 8g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 1/2 cup rolled oats
   - 1 cup water or milk
   - 1 ripe banana, sliced
   - 1 tbsp honey
   - 1/4 tsp cinnamon (optional)
   Preparation:
   1. Combine oats and liquid in a pot
   2. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally
   3. Cook for 5 minutes until thickened
   4. Top with sliced banana, honey, and cinnamon
   Difficulty: Easy
   Prep time: 8 minutes
   Dietary notes: Whole grain, good source of fiber, natural sweetness from fruit

3. Greek Yogurt with Granola and Berries

4. Whole Wheat Pancakes with Maple Syrup
   Calories: 420
   Macros: 12g protein, 10g fat, 70g carbs, 7g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 1 cup whole wheat flour
   - 1 tbsp baking powder
   - 1 tbsp sugar
   - 1 egg
   - 1 cup milk
   - 2 tbsp vegetable oil
   - 2 tbsp maple syrup
   Preparation:
   1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl
   2. Whisk egg, milk, and oil in another bowl
   3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed
   4. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat
   5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake
   6. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook other side
   7. Serve with maple syrup
   Difficulty: Medium
   Prep time: 20 minutes
   Dietary notes: Whole grain, can be made dairy-free with plant milk

7. Omelet with Spinach and Mushrooms
   Calories: 280
   Macros: 20g protein, 18g fat, 8g carbs, 3g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 2 large eggs
   - 1 cup fresh spinach
   - 1/4 cup mushrooms, sliced
   - 1 tbsp olive oil
   - 1 oz cheese (optional)
   - Salt and pepper to taste
   Preparation:
   1. Beat eggs with salt and pepper
   2. Heat oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat
   3. Sauté mushrooms until softened
   4. Add spinach and cook until wilted
   5. Pour in beaten eggs
   6. Cook until edges set, then fold in half
   7. Add cheese if desired and cook until eggs are set
   Difficulty: Medium
   Prep time: 15 minutes
   Dietary notes: Low carb, high protein, keto-friendly, gluten-free

8. Breakfast Burrito with Eggs, Cheese, and Veggies
   Calories: 450
   Macros: 22g protein, 20g fat, 40g carbs, 6g fiber
   Ingredients:
   - 1 large whole wheat tortilla
   - 2 eggs, scrambled
   - 1/4 cup bell peppers, diced
   - 2 tbsp onions, diced
   - 1/4 cup black beans (optional)
   - 1/4 cup shredded cheese
   - 1 tbsp salsa
   - 1 tsp olive oil
   Preparation:
   1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat
   2. Sauté peppers and onions until softened
   3. Add eggs and scramble until cooked
   4. Warm tortilla in microwave or on a dry pan
   5. Place egg mixture, beans, cheese, and salsa on tortilla
   6. Fold sides and roll up tightly
   Difficulty: Medium
   Prep time: 15 minutes
   Dietary notes: Portable breakfast, good source of protein and fiber

13. Whole Wheat Waffles with Fruit Compote
    Calories: 450
    Macros: 12g protein, 15g fat, 70g carbs, 8g fiber
    Ingredients:
    - 1 cup whole wheat flour
    - 1 tbsp baking powder
    - 1 tbsp sugar
    - 1 egg
    - 1 cup milk
    - 2 tbsp vegetable oil
    - 1 cup mixed berries
    - 2 tbsp maple syrup
    Preparation:
    1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl
    2. Whisk egg, milk, and oil in another bowl
    3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed

    Prep time: 25 minutes
    Dietary notes: Whole grain, rich in antioxidants from berries
